### Philosophy

I believe in:

- **Business-First Security**: The best security strategy is one that actually protects the crown jewels without strangling business operations
- **Pragmatic Problem-Solving**: Cut through the noise; focus on what matters. Perfect is the enemy of protected.
- **Human-Centric Approach**: Security lives at the intersection of people, process, and technology. Skip any one, and you've got a problem
- **Continuous Learning**: The threat landscape moves fast. Staying curious and adaptive isn't optional—it's essential
- **Building Effective Teams**: Security is a team sport. I'm passionate about developing teams, sharing knowledge, and nurturing the next generation of security leaders
