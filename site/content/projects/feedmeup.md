feedmeup is a fully automated news pipeline that fetches from 40+ RSS feeds across AI, Cloud, Cybersecurity, and APAC regulatory domains, deduplicates articles with fuzzy matching, and generates AI-powered analyst summaries — all published on a schedule via GitHub Actions to a static Astro site. The vision is a zero-maintenance intelligence briefing that surfaces signal from noise so you never miss what matters.
